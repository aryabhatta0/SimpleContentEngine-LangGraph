Sports in India have a rich and diverse history, with various traditional and modern sports being played across the country. 
Among these, cricket stands out as the most popular and widely followed sport. 
Cricket in India is not just a game; it's a passion that unites millions of fans. 
The Indian Premier League (IPL) has further boosted the sport's popularity, attracting international players and massive viewership. 
Other sports like hockey, badminton, and football also have significant followings, with Indian athletes achieving success in various international competitions. 
Despite the dominance of cricket, efforts are being made to promote a wider range of sports to ensure holistic development and greater representation on the global stage.
